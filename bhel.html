Bhel Puri

Ingredients
Murmura/puffed rice - 5 cups, Onion- 1, Tomato- 1, Cilantro, Sev - 4 tbsp, Salt, Green chilli paste- 1 tbsp, Grated carrot - 1/2 cup, Cucumber- 1/2, Olive oil- 1 tsp.

Take the murmura in a big bowl. If the murmura is not crispy, place it in the microwave for a minute. to the bowl, add salt, sev, green chilli paste, oil and mix well. Now add grated carrot, choppe onion, chopped tomato, chopped cilantro and serve immediately.
