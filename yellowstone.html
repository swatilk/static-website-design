Yellowstone National Park

Yellowstone National Park is the world's first national park and one of the largest in the United States.Yellowstone is home to several hot springs and a diversity of wildlife. The most famous hot spring is the 'Old Faithful'. It gets the name because it erupts almost at the same time every day 'faithfully'. Yellowstone has many such geysers that are accessible through your car. Also there is a good chance that you spot bison, pronghorns, moose, elk or bear while driving. although bears are not said to be seen close to the road but there is no denial. We did spot few bisons, elk, pronghorns and moose. It is never adviced to approach any animals even though they look harmless.

Yellowstone has beautiful waterfalls, lower falls, upper falls to name a few. There are some amazing cabins to stay while you are visiting. There are many activities like camping, boating that you can do while at yellowstone, but few need permit. Make sure to keep things where they are and stay on trail.Have an awesome trip!
