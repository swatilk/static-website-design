Aloo Paratha

Ingredients
Whole wheat flour - 1 cup, Water - 1/2 cup, Salt, Boiled Potaoes- 2, Onion - 1, Cilantro, Coriander seeds - 1 tsp, Garam Masala - 1/2 tsp, Chaat Masala - 1 tsp, Green chillis - 2, Oil - 2 tsp

For the filling, mash the boiled potatoes in a bowl, add chopped onion, chopped cilantro, garam masala, salt, coriander seeds, chaat masala, chopped green chillis and mix well. Now prepare the dough by mixing wheat flour and salt and water. Add water little at a time and make a smooth dough; keep it aside. After 20 minutes, take a lemon sized ball of dough, roll it out, fill the stuffing and pack it like we cover dumplings. Roll out to make a roti/paratha. Bake this on hot griddle for 3 minutes on each side with 1/2 tsp oil.
